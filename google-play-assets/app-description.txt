Rose Wallet - Automatic Expense Tracker

Track your expenses automatically by reading SMS messages from Indian banks. No manual data entry required!

KEY FEATURES:
• Automatic SMS parsing from major Indian banks
• Real-time expense tracking and categorization  
• Budget management with visual progress indicators
• Privacy-first: All data stored locally on your device
• Offline functionality - no internet required
• Support for UPI, IMPS, NEFT, RTGS transactions
• Pattern recognition for distinct transaction types
• Export transaction data and reports

HOW IT WORKS:
The app automatically detects and parses bank SMS messages to extract transaction details like amount, merchant name, date, and account information. It categorizes expenses and provides insights into your spending patterns.

PERFECT FOR:
• Users who want automatic expense tracking
• People who prefer privacy (no cloud storage)
• Anyone tired of manual data entry
• Users with multiple bank accounts

PRIVACY & SECURITY:
• All data stays on your device
• No data sent to external servers
• Secure local storage
• Full control over your financial data
